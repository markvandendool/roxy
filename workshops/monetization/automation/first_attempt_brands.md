# Brand Options: "First Attempt" Vibe
## Honest, Self-Aware, Learning-in-Public

### Top Picks

1. **FirstAttempt**
   - Username: @firstattempt or @first.attempt
   - Email: firstattempt@proton.me
   - Bio: "Documenting my first attempt at everything: AI workflows, automation, content creation. Here's what worked (and what exploded)."
   - Vibe: Ultra-honest, self-deprecating, relatable
   - Why it works: Direct, memorable, sets expectation that failures are part of the content

2. **AttemptOne**
   - Username: @attempt.one or @attemptn1
   - Email: attemptn1@proton.me
   - Bio: "Attempt #1 at posting automation. Attempt #47 at not breaking my GPU. Guitar teacher learning AI. Join the chaos."
   - Vibe: Playful numbering (but not AI-sloppy), implies iteration
   - Why it works: Sounds like version control, tech people get it

3. **StillLearning**
   - Username: @stilllearning or @still.learning
   - Email: stilllearning@proton.me
   - Bio: "Still learning AI, still learning automation, still learning not to rm -rf my entire system. Sharing the journey."
   - Vibe: Humble, continuous growth, honest
   - Why it works: Everyone's still learning, hyper-relatable

4. **FirstPost**
   - Username: @firstpost or @first.post
   - Email: firstpost@proton.me
   - Bio: "My first post about automation. And second. And third. Documenting the learning curve from guitar teacher to AI tinkerer."
   - Vibe: Simple, direct, content-focused
   - Why it works: Clear what the account is about, no pretense

5. **RoughDraft**
   - Username: @roughdraft or @rough.draft
   - Email: roughdraft@proton.me
   - Bio: "Rough drafts of AI workflows, automation scripts, creative tech. Nothing polished. Everything documented."
   - Vibe: Creative + tech fusion, embraces imperfection
   - Why it works: Appeals to creators and developers, "draft" implies authenticity

6. **TrialRun**
   - Username: @trialrun or @trial.run
   - Email: trialrun@proton.me
   - Bio: "Trial runs in AI automation. Some work. Some crash spectacularly. All shared. Guitar teacher gone tech."
   - Vibe: Experimental, scientific approach, honest about failures
   - Why it works: "Trial and error" angle, fits the learning narrative

7. **WorkInProgress**
   - Username: @workinprogress or @wip.tech
   - Email: wip@proton.me
   - Bio: "WIP: Building AI workflows. WIP: Automating content. WIP: Not destroying hardware. Guitar + tech experiments."
   - Vibe: Transparent about incompleteness, process-focused
   - Why it works: Everyone respects the WIP tag, signals ongoing journey

8. **TakeOne**
   - Username: @take.one or @takeone
   - Email: takeone@proton.me
   - Bio: "Take 1: Here's my automation stack. Take 47: Still fixing the bugs. Guitar teacher documenting the tech rabbit hole."
   - Vibe: Film/music production reference, iterative
   - Why it works: Natural for content creation + music background

### Recommendation: **FirstAttempt** or **RoughDraft**

**FirstAttempt** if you want maximum honesty/relatability  
**RoughDraft** if you want creative + tech fusion

Both:
- No numbers (anti-AI-slop)
- Self-aware without being apologetic
- Natural content angles ("Here's attempt #1 at...", "Rough draft of my workflow...")
- Memorable and searchable
- Work across all platforms
